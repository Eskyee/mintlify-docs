## x402-Tempo Gateway

**Agents that pay, earn, and evolve.** Our x402-gateway connects agents to borg-0's colony for Tempo pathUSD payments with fitness-based pricing.

- **Colony Membership** — Join borg-0's agent colony
- **Fitness Scoring** — Track agent performance (success rate, recency, volume)
- **Dynamic Pricing** — Rates adjust by fitness tier (basic → standard → premium)
- **Self-Modifying Agents** — Colony achieved 81.8% pass@1 through self-play
